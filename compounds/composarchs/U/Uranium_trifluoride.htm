Synthesis

Uranium trifluoride can be obtained by reacting uranium(IV) fluoride with aluminium at 900 °C or with uranium:[3]

U F 4 + A l ⟶ U F 3 + A l F {\displaystyle \mathrm {UF_{4}+Al\longrightarrow UF_{3}+AlF} }
3   U F 4 + U ⟶ 4   U F 3 {\displaystyle \mathrm {3\ UF_{4}+U\longrightarrow 4\ UF_{3}} }
