Paradów

Paradów [paˈraduf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Pogorzela, within Gostyń County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland.[1] It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) south-east of Pogorzela, 21 km (13 mi) south-east of Gostyń, and 73 km (45 mi) south of the regional capital Poznań.
