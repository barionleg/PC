Dihydrogossypetin

Dihydrogossypetin is a flavanonol, a type of flavonoid.[1]

Biosynthesis

The enzyme Taxifolin 8-monooxygenase hydroxylates taxifolin using NADH, NADPH, H+, and O2 to produce 2,3-dihydrogossypetin, NAD+, NADP+, and H2O.
