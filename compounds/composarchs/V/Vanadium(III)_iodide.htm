Vanadium(III) iodide

Vanadium(III) iodide is the inorganic compound with the formula VI3. This paramagnetic solid is generated by the reaction of vanadium powder with iodine at around 500 Â°C.[1] The black hygroscopic crystals dissolve in water to give green solutions, characteristic of V(III) ions.
